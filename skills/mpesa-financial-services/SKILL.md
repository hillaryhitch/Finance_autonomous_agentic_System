## Overview
Deep expertise in M-PESA mobile money platform, financial services, and fintech ecosystem including transactions, lending, savings, and payment services.

## Platform Overview

### Scale and Reach
- Kenya's largest mobile money platform
- Over 30 million active users
- Market-leading position in mobile financial services
- Integrated into Kenya's economic infrastructure

### Core Services

#### Money Transfer
- Person-to-person transfers
- Wallet-to-wallet transactions
- Cash-in and cash-out at agent network
- Real-time settlement

#### Payment Services
- **Lipa Na M-PESA**: Merchant payment solution
- Bill payments (utilities, government, services)
- Online payment gateway
- API integrations for businesses

#### Lending Products
- **Fuliza**: Overdraft facility for customers
- **M-Shwari**: Savings and loan product (partnership with NCBA Bank)
- Credit scoring based on transaction history
- Instant loan disbursement

#### Savings Products
- M-Shwari savings accounts
- Interest-earning deposits
- Goal-based savings features

#### International Services
- International remittances
- Cross-border money transfers
- Partnership with Western Union and other providers

## Financial Performance

### Revenue Model
- Transaction fees (graduated based on amount)
- Loan interest income
- Merchant commissions
- API and integration fees

### Contribution to Safaricom
- M-PESA contributes approximately 35-40% of Safaricom's total service revenue
- High-margin business with strong growth trajectory
- Strategic importance beyond direct revenue

## Ecosystem and Integrations

### Regulatory Bodies

### Compliance Requirements

- **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)**: Transaction monitoring and reporting
- **Data Protection**: Customer data privacy and security
- **Consumer Protection**: Fair practices and dispute resolution
