## Overview
Expert knowledge of Safaricom's telecommunications business including mobile voice, data, messaging, and network infrastructure across Kenya, Ethiopia, and regional operations.

## Core Business

### Market Position
- Safaricom is East Africa's leading telecommunications provider
- Dominant player in Kenya with approximately 65% market share
- Listed on Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)

### Core Services
- **Mobile Voice**: Traditional voice calling services
- **Mobile Data**: 3G, 4G, and 5G data services
- **SMS**: Text messaging services
- **Network Infrastructure**: Extensive network coverage across Kenya

### Regional Operations

#### Kenya Operations
- Primary market with mature infrastructure
- Extensive rural and urban coverage
- Strong brand recognition and customer loyalty

#### Ethiopia Operations
- Operating through Safaricom Telecommunications Ethiopia (STE)
- New market entry with significant investment requirements
- Greenfield deployment of telecommunications infrastructure
- Competitive landscape with Ethio Telecom as incumbent

## Competitive Landscape

### Key Competitors in Kenya
- **Airtel Kenya**: Second-largest operator
- **Telkom Kenya**: Third player, smaller market share

### Key Competitors in Ethiopia
- **Ethio Telecom**: State-owned incumbent operator
- New market with evolving competitive dynamics

## Regulatory Environment

### Kenya
- Regulated by Communications Authority of Kenya (CA)
- License fees and spectrum obligations
- Consumer protection regulations
- Data privacy requirements under Data Protection Act

### Ethiopia
- Regulated by Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA)
- National Bank of Ethiopia for financial services
- Evolving regulatory framework for new entrants

## Strategic Focus
- Network quality and coverage leadership
- 5G rollout in urban centers
- Fiber optic expansion for fixed broadband
- Digital services and innovation
- Regional expansion strategy
